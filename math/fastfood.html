FastFood

Given $n$ prizes, denote their values by $$v_1, v_2, \ldots, v_n.$$ Denote the number of available stickers for prize $i$, sticker type $j$ as $R_{i,j}$.

Then the total amount $A$ of prize money is given by $$A = \sum_{i=1}^n v_i ( \min_{j} R_{i,j} ).$$

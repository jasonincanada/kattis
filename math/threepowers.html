Three Powers

Consider the set $S_b$ of non-negative integer powers of $b$: $$ S_b := \{ b^0, b^1, b^2, b^3, \ldots \} $$ For $b = 3$: $$ S_3 := \{ 1, 3, 9, 27, \ldots \} $$ The $n$th subset of $S_b$ when listed in the order of the sum of the values in the subset is given by: $$ S_{b,n} := \bigcup_{i=0}^{\left\lfloor log_2 n \right\rfloor} \begin{cases} \{ b^i \}, &\text{if $ \left\lfloor \frac{n-1}{2^i} \right\rfloor \mod 2 = 1 $}\\ \emptyset, &\text{otherwise} \end{cases} $$

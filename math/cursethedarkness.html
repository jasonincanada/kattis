Curse The Darkness

Given candles $C := \{ c_1, c_2, \ldots, c_n \}$, denote their x and y coordinates by $x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n$ and $y_1, y_2, \ldots, y_n$ respectively. Let our book's coordinates be $b_x$ and $b_y$. Let $x_i, y_i, b_x, b_y \in \mathbb{R}$.

The set $\gamma$ of candles within 8 meters of our book is given by $$\gamma := \{ c_i \in C \space|\space \sqrt{(x_i - b_x)^2 + (y_i - b_y)^2} \le 8 \}.$$ The answer $A$ to the problem is given by $$A := \begin{cases} \text{light a candle}, & \gamma \ne \emptyset \\ \text{curse the darkness}, & otherwise \end{cases}$$
